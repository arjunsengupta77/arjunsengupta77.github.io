About Mariners Base Camp

Passionately supporting the club since 2016

We are the biggest fan club of Mohun Bagan in the country. We repesent the voice of millions of supporters in Kolkata and across the world. Our organization started off in 2016 with just 10 members and now we have grown to be a family of more than 20000. We are passionate. We are relentless. We will stop at nothing to defend the glory, honour and legacy of our beloved club. We are Mariners Base Camp!
